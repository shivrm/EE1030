\textbf{Question: }\\
Find the unit vector in the direction of $PQ$, where $\vec P$ and
$\vec Q$ have coordinates $(5, 0, 8)$ and $(3, 3, 2)$, respectively.

\textbf{Solution: } \\
Given that
$$
\vec P = \myvec{5 \\ 0 \\ 8}, \vec Q = \myvec{3 \\ 3 \\ 2}
$$

The unit vector in the direction of $PQ$ is

\begin{align}
\frac{\vec{Q} - \vec{P}}{\norm{\vec{Q} - \vec{P}}} = \frac{1}{7} \myvec{-2 \\ 3 \\ -6}
\end{align}
